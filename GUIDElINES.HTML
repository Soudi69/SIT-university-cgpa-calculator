Guide Lines!

This software is designed for the following purpose:
  • To Calculate the accurate C.I.E of each Subject as per the semester
  • To Give accurate sum of Credits
  • To Find out Grade of each given Subject
  • Last but Not least: To display the RESULT SHEET which contains:
    1. 'Grade Points' along with Subject name,Subject Code,CIE,Credits and Grade
    2. SGPA
    3. Percentage
    4. RESULT!

How To Use?
To use this software you need to have all the required information, such as:
[optional]=> student name,subject code,subject name,CIE.
Mandatory => Number Of subjects, Credits given for each subject,For CIE Calculation (Credits of: Quiz 1..Quiz 4,Assinment,Test 1 & Test 2) & Last: SEE marks!

After Collecting the requirements: Proceed Forward and enter the basic details such as student name, USN,Semester in the provided space

Click on 'Total Semester Credits': And Enter the Subject information in the provided space and click on 'Total Credits' to find the sum of all credits!
Now To Calculate the CIE: click on 'CIE' scroll down to access the CIE calculation Table. Enter the necessary details and then click on
  • 'Solve'(Down) =>To calculate a particular subject CIE
  • 'Compute All CIE'=> To compute all the entered subjects CIE

If you already have the CIE of all subjects: just click on 'Subject Grade' (To find grades for each subject)
if Your subjects CIE is a calculated(in CIE Table) Then Click on 'Copy Values' to Copy the Credits,CIE,& Subjects names

Calculating SEE Grade: To Calculate the Grade for Each subject Enter the "SEE Marks" and "CIE"
Next is to Get the Result Sheet, Enter the Subject Credits ('Copy Values' button=>To Copy all Credits directly)
Click on 'Calculate SEE Grade' button to preform all calculations and Last is to click on the "RESULT SHEET" to obtain your result!, That's it!
Extra Buttons:
  • 'Clear'=> Clears all Tables
  • 'Clear All'=> Clears the entire page values

Hope You feel comfortable with it!
